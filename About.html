Biography

Over the past few years, I thought I knew exactly where my life was headed. It was simple: I would work in market research and statistics until the day I inevitably could not work any longer. I was very content in pursuing this path, as well.

When I graduated college with my undergrad in 2020, business and statics where all I knew. I started my career a year before as an early hire at a market research firm in Indianapolis, Indiana, ready to fulfill my dream and act on my passions. I did exactly that for the next two years.

However, I couldn't contain my need for creativity. Around the same time in mid-2019, I co-founded successful online digital marketing firm, CHOICE, LLP, as I saw a growing need for elder-care and related accessable resouces. Before I had a great desire to be a developer, I created, hosted, and administered the CHOICE, LLP website, and continued to work as the Chief Analytics and Operations Manager alongside my full-time position as a Research Analyst. It would be an understatement to say I enjoyed both opportunities, but over the next two years, I developed a strong interest to combine my experience and passions. I wanted to do something more; I felt as if I didn't have the competiticeny to do any more, however. In late 2020, I left my position in market research and sold what little stake I had left in CHOICE, LLP. I knew in order to take control and effectively combine my experience and passions with the freedom and creativity I desired, the next best path to pursue was a career in technology.

In late 2020, I started a career as a Quality Assurance Engineer for a local software company in Indianapolis, Indiana. When I started, I over-saw testing for our newest product, a mobile-first point of sale. I immedaitely fell in love with the position, the company, and the industry. Since my start in software, a new passion for software development has evolved to couple my passoins for business marketing, statics, and entrepreneurship. While my path to becoming a full stack software developer has been a little unorthodox, I wouldn't change the path I've taken for anything.

I made the decision to enroll at Eleven Fifty Academy to pursue a greater knowledge in software development. After graduating, my confidence in my competency as a full stack software developer has never been higher.

Moving forward, I have a growing list of aspirations I intend to pursue, and am excited at the thought of any fulfilling future opportunity.

Isaac Tillema
