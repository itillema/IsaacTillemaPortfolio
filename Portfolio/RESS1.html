RESS1

Description

RESS1 (Real-Estate Strategy Software, First-Gen) is an ASP.NET MVC application designed for real-estate investors and property management companies to better create and utilize investment and management strategies.

Currently, there is no fully comprehensive software specifically for property investors to use in order to make strategic property investment decisions. What's more, is that most property investors and investment companies invest for the sole purpose of renting out their properties for profit or passive income to pay for other properties.

RESS1 fills this gap by allowing investors to select properties they wish to invest in and running various strategic analyses that property investors care about most to better decide whether a property(s) is a valid purchase, given a wide variety of variables. Once investors have made the move to purchase properties, RESS1 allows them to keep track of property conditions, tenants, other property owners (clients), and then run management analyses to decide if their investments are paying off, or if a property is worth selling.
