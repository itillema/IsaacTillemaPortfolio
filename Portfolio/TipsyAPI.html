TIPSYAPI

Description

Have you ever been at the bar on a crowded Saturday night? How difficult was it to clearly articulate to the bartender or waiter/waitress exactly what you wanted to drink? TipsyAPI has a solution to this issue.

Created as part of a group project for Eleven Fifty Academy, TipsyAPI is a highly adaptable and transferable ASP.NET WebAPI application, initially designed for bars/pubs to take drink orders without interacting with bartenders, servers, or hostesses.

This application utilizes N-tier architecture and a WebAPI, and was made in collaboration with Brandon Bautista.
